# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers lead user research, design, and usability efforts, focusing on creating optimal user experiences that address user needs and business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Develop wireframes, prototypes, and visual design assets
- Advocate for user needs in planning and reviews
- Collaborate with product, developers, and QA on feature specs

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and usable interfaces
- Drive continuous UX improvement via feedback

### Interactions
- Works closely with Product Managers on requirements
- Provides design assets and feedback to Developers
- Engages with QA to review usability and accessibility

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define metrics, ensure data quality, monitor outcomes, and deliver insights to drive product and business decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Define success and usage metrics for features
- Assess, clean, and validate data
- Analyze results and produce reports/insights
- Work with PM/engineers to instrument analytics

### Goals
- Provide actionable insight that improves product outcomes
- Ensure metrics align with project goals

### Interactions
- Partners with Product Managers to clarify goals and metrics
- Supports reporting for stakeholders
- May help QA/Product resolve questions about success/failure

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers ensure build/deploy pipeline reliability and enable fast, safe releases through automation, monitoring, and collaboration.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain build, deploy, and CI/CD pipelines
- Monitor system reliability and performance
- Develop and refine incident/rollback processes
- Support reliability/security in releases

### Goals
- Drive release stability and velocity
- Reduce manual toil in development handoffs

### Interactions
- Supports Developers and QA during testing and releases
- Coordinates with Project Managers on deployment schedules and incident response

---

## Customer Support / Success

### Role Summary
Support/Success team members gather user feedback, triage post-release issues, and help communicate between end users and the project team.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and surface user feedback
- Triage support issues for product/development review
- Communicate known issues and workarounds to users
- Document common issues and solutions

### Goals
- Improve user satisfaction and drive continuous improvement
- Serve as key input to roadmap discussions

### Interactions
- Provides 'voice of the customer' to Product and Project Managers
- Supports incident/rollback response efforts

---

- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
